Hot Beans Web

Who we are

Hot Beans Web was formed in 2014 by brothers Mark and Stewart from the modest surroundings of their West Bridgford family home. After graduating in Computer Science from Leicester University in 2013 the brothers initially began building websites for the businesses of family and friends, their passion and talent as web developers quickly saw their business expand.


Now based in a modern and vibrant office in the centre of Nottingham, the company has grown to more than 100 associates and our mission is to provide a collaborative and exciting workplace that allows our developers to shine.


We are an award winning and innovative web design agency working with some of the best small to medium sized businesses in the Midlands. We work closely with our clients to create a brief for the design of informative, enticing and bespoke websites.


But we don't just leave it there as we offer a full management package to allow for content and format updates to ensure the website continues to evolve and adapt to the client's changing business needs.

Careers

What we can offer you

At Hot Beans Web we employ some of the best web developers in the industry and are always on the lookout for fresh new talent to join us.


If you have recently completed qualifications or training courses in web development and are now looking for a position in a dynamic, creative and forward-thinking company then Hot Beans Web is for you.


At Hot Beans Web we are committed to the development of your professional and personal growth. To do this we offer ongoing training to industry standards as well as continued support and opportunities to progress. Many of our web developers started with us as trainees and are now in charge of their own projects and teams.


We offer a great range of benefits including a competitive salary, an excellent holiday package, flexible hours and remote working.

Who are we looking for

At Hot Beans Web we want to recruit the best people and high performers who thrive in a pulsating and fast-moving environment. You will be someone who leads by example, who can build strong and valuable relationships and have the confidence to influence others. We are looking for problem solvers who are results focussed and have the ability to use their judgment in order to get the job done. You will work as part of a self-organised team of developers so you will need to be an enthusiastic team player who enjoys collaborating with colleagues to share ideas and best practices. We invest heavily in developing great people and while you will have the skills and expertise, we are looking for you will also be keen to learn and be committed to develop and build on your existing talent.

Apply to Us

Junior web developers are responsible for assisting the web development team in all aspects of website and application design This will include working with clients to both ascertain needs and to update on progress • assisting other team members in the plan and design process of a website • assisting with the build and structure of a website • writing and editing website content. • writing code in one or more programming or scripting language, ensuring it meets industry standards • implementing new design features including the conversion of files into web compatible formats • assisting with the testing and reviewing of both front and backend applications • maintaining webpages and performing updates • reviewing colleagues work • keeping up to date with latest technology and programming trends.

A solid understanding of website design and development principles is essential. This will include being skilled in the use of HTML and CSS with a good understanding of JavaScript. An understanding of other programming languages would be beneficial. • competent in testing at both a functional and unit level, being able to consistently and effectively test and rectify errors is essential • excellent design skills with an attention to detail, an understanding of how responsive design works is also vital • a knowledge of back-end web development is desirable including some familiarity with how servers work, APIs, SQL, databases and security protocols • an understanding of Search Engine Optimisation • good interpersonal skills including excellent analytical skills, good written and verbal communication and the ability to work collaboratively • ability to work under pressure to tight deadlines without compromising on quality.

A level 5 (HND) or a level 6 qualification (Degree) in Web Development, or a related field is essential • any other relevant courses or certification and/or experience of web development is desirable.
